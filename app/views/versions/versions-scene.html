1.0.1

Added multiple card support to threads.
Now you can open a thread in a new card. To do it, just do a metatap (gesture area+tap on thread simultaneously), or tap and hold over a thread and select "Open in new Card".

Forums handles automatically sessions: if you open a thread in a new card, and log in the forum from that card, all the opened cards on the forum will be automatically logged in, and keeps sesion alive in all cards.

Also, you don't need to worry about closing each card. If you want to close a forum entirely, just throw away the full forum card. All the child cards will be closed automatically.

As always, you can keep open all the forums you want with all the child cards you need. This feature works on all the thread lists: Recent Posts, Unread Posts, Subscriptions, Participated topics and Search.

We are working to enable this feature with subforums too.

Improved emoticons support
We have added some new emoticons and also improved the emoticons handling. Each update will add new emoticons, but please be patient with this: we are trying to provide an unique experience with this, not just copy that old styled board emoticons.
