Debug
These options are not sticky. They will turn themselves back off when you close the app. This is for performance and security reasons.
Detailed logging
Activating this option, the app will write detailed logging info for troubleshooting problems. It will slow down the app in some places - some more than others.

Log password
Activating this option, the app will write your forum password to the log in PLAIN TEXT (not encrypted) for troubleshooting login problems. It will NOT transmit your password over the network in unencrypted, only to the system message log. Do not enable this unless you know you need to, or just have no fear. :)
